<h1 id="about-me"><span class="lang-en">😊 About Me</span><span class="lang-zh">😊 关于我</span></h1>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at the School of Computer Science, Peking University, under the guidance of Professors Wenpin Jiao and Zhi Jin in the Key Laboratory of High Confidence Software Technologies. Previously, I obtained my B.S. degree in Software Engineering from Southwest University. My research interests include Multi-Modal Learning, Generative Artificial Intelligence, Trustworthy Machine Learning and Spatial Intelligence.

</div>

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">

我目前在北京大学计算机学院攻读博士学位,导师为焦文品教授与金芝教授,所在实验室为高可信软件技术教育部重点实验室。本科毕业于西南大学软件工程专业。研究兴趣包括多模态学习、生成式人工智能、可信机器学习与空间智能。
